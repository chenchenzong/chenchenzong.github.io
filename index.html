About Me

I am Chen-Chen Zong, a Ph.D. student at College of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), advised by Prof. Sheng-Jun Huang.

Prior to my Ph.D. endeavor, I obtained my Bachelor’s degree in 2021 at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA). I was initially enrolled in the Master’s program and later transitioned to a Ph.D. program via an integrated M.S.-Ph.D. track in April 2023. I have been fortunate to receive consistent guidance from Prof. Sheng-Jun Huang since 2019.

Research Interest

My research interests focus primarily on weakly supervised learning under complex and open-world settings. Currently, I am especially interested in active learning and noisy label learning within both open-set and federated learning scenarios. In addition, I closely follow topics such as uncertainty quantification and knowledge distillation.

I am always open to research collaborations. If you are interested, feel free to reach out to me via email.
